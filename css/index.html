Dylan Conklin is a designer, developer, real human being and a real hero.

My favorite things have always been the simplest.


Ribeye with dill butter, Fender Telecasters and Chuck Taylor sneakers. Maybe these things are inherently good, maybe they are just true to themselves. Either way, i'm that kind of guy.

I love good typography and grid layouts. I believe web experiences travel beyond the desktop and need to look great on any platform or device.

I design and develop responsive, grid based web sites that are easy to look at and easy to use. I don't design for other designers, I design for web users.

Work

  • NaturalGas.is

    NaturalGas.is is an educational initiative of Chesapeake Energy. This site was created to show all the uses, benefits and facts relating to natural gas usage. This site served as the home-base for a video and social campaign which receives heavy rotation nation wide, In addition to the video's prime placement at OKC Thunder games, this site is also the recipient of a gold 2012 ADDY's award in the interactive category.

    Design, HTML5, CSS3, Jquery

  • Peake Fuel Solutions

    This project is under construction. Peake Fuel Solutions is an affiliate of Chesapeake Energy, they offer a product to fleet and fueling stations that allows them to add CNG pumps to their location easily with their CNG In A Box™ product. they wanted a responsive site with an easy to use CNG conversion calculator to show the cost benefits of CNG.

    Design, HTML5, CSS3, Jquery, Bootstrap

  • DinkYardSale.com

    I created this website to market my legendary annual yard sale. The began when we were expecting our son and we needed to make room. This website was the cornerstone of a successful social campaign on facebook and twitter. Each year we sell out. A previous years design was featured on The Oklahoma City Egotist

    Brand Creation, Design, HTML5, CSS3, Jquery, Foundation, Google API

  • Designatr.com

    The client needed a landing page for their upcoming web application during the development phase. I was brought in at the very beginning to create the brand and provide art direction, branding and collateral material. The project later evolved into something entirely different, however, this landing page was featured on smashingmagazine.com in an article on designing stunning "Coming Soon" pages. (Scroll to the bottom)

    Brand Creation, Art Direction, Collateral

  • Cafe501

    I worked with the client and a small local agency to create a mouth-watering website that incorporated the beautiful photography of their new location in Classen Curve and their appetizing fare.

    HTML5, CSS3, Jquery

  • Monscierge

    This was the outward facing website for a startup where I served as Creative Director and Lead Designer from 2009-2011. The company specialized in large platform touch screen interfaces and software for the hospitality industry. I worked with my team to create the branding, web layout you see here and content for this website and the backend CMS.

    Brand Creation, Art Direction, Collateral, HTML5, CSS, Jquery, Web Strategy

  • Monscierge CMS

    In addition to the outward facing website, I also designed the backend CMS for our hospitality clients. This CMS allowed the users to fully manipulate their screen content with custom recommendations, images and special offers.

    Design, Icon Design, HTML5, CSS3, Jquery, Salesforce
